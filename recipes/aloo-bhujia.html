Aloo Bhujia Recipe

Description

One of my favorite and easy to make vegetarian dishes. I learnt this dish when I was studying abroad in Costa Rica, and have improved upon the original recipe through my Mom's suggestions.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (1 kg diced)
  • Onion (1/2 small/medium finely diced)
  • Tomatoes (2 small finely chopped)
  • Bell Pepper (1 finely diced without seeds)
  • Mushrooms (4 medium finely diced - Optional)
  • Shan Aloo Bhujia Masala (2 tbsp)
  • Coriander (3 tbsp finely chopped)
  • Dried Red Chilli (1 to 3 based on chili tolerance/preference )
  • Turmeric (1/2 tsp)
  • Ground Cloves (1/2 tsp)
  • Cumin Seeds (1/2 tsp)
  • Oil (1 to 2 tbsp)

Steps

  1. Add everything into a pot
  2. Heat up the pot while occasionally stirring
  3. When the pot heats up, put a lid on top and reduce the heat to low/low-medium
  4. Let the potatoes simmer for 30 - 45 minutes before opening the lid and mixing everything again
  5. Close the lid again and let the potatoes simmer for longer if they aren't cooked all the way through
  6. Once the potatoes are cooked, serve the meal with some naan
