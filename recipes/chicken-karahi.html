Chicken Karahi Recipe

Description

A trademark of Pakistani cuisine, this exquisite dish is the perfect combination of savory and spicy. It is the gateway to start your journey into Pakistani food and culture.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (1 kg)
  • Onion (1/2 medium)
  • Ginger (1 tbsp)
  • Garlic (1 tbsp)
  • Shan Chicken Karahi Masala (1 tbsp)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Green Chillies (2/3 chopped)
  • Soy Sauce (2 tbsp)
  • Vinegar (1 tbsp)
  • Black Pepper (2 tbsp)
  • Coriander (2/3 tbsp chopped)
  • Tomatoes (2 medium/small diced)
  • Oil (2/3 tbsp)

Steps

  1. Heat up oil in a pan on medium-high
  2. Caramelize onions in hot oil
  3. Add ginger, garlic, and chicken in with the onions and turn the stove to high heat
  4. Once the chicken is slightly golden seared, add tomatoes
  5. When the tomatoes have cooked down into a liquid, add the soy sauce, vinegar, Shan Chicken Karahi Masala, Salt, and Black Pepper.
  6. Reduce gravy to a thicker consistency on high heat while constantly stirring.
  7. When gravy at desired consistency, reduce the heat to low, add green chillies and coriander, and let the dish simmer with a lid on for 5 minutes.
  8. The dish is best enjoyed with naan! Bon appetit
