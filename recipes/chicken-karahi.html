Chicken Karahi Recipe

Description

A trademark of Pakistani cuisine, this exquisite dish is the perfect combination of savory and spicy. It is the gateway to start your journey into Pakistani food and culture.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (1 kg)
  • Onion (1/2 medium)
  • Ginger (1 tbsp)
  • Garlic (1 tbsp)
  • Shan Chicken Karahi Masala (1 tbsp)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Green Chillies (2/3 chopped)
  • Soy Sauce (2 tbsp)
  • Vinegar (1 tbsp)
  • Black Pepper (2 tbsp)
  • Coriander (2/3 tbsp chopped)
  • Tomatoes (2 medium/small diced)
