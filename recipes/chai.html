Chai Recipe

Description

This heavenly beverage is a treat for anyone who wants to enhance their day.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Black Tea (I prefer Tapal Danedar loose leaf tea)
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom (I prefer using powder, but you can use pods as well)
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Add 1 mug (350 ml) of milk into a pot
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of loose leaf Black Tea
  3. Add half to a quarter of a whole Cinnamon Stick
  4. Add quarter teaspoon of Cardamom
  5. Add 2 teaspoons of Sugar
  6. Turn stove on to High Heat and bring everything to boil while stirring (be careful as the milk will rise and can spill out of the pot)
  7. When the milk begins to rise, turn the heat down to low/low-medium and let the tea simmer for 5 minutes (if you want a richer flavor, let it simmer for 15 minutes)
  8. Use a strainer to remove tea leaves, cinnamon stick, and cardamom
  9. Drink and enjoy!
